    • RCB'S GREEN INITIATIVES

    Waste Management at the Chinnaswamy stadium

    Food, Plastic and Paper result in tonnes of waste every match. RCB in association with Goodera and Saahas will segregate and recycle the waste that's generated and collected at the stadium during our home matches.

    Rejuvenation of Lakes in Bengaluru

    RCB in partnership with Goodera will be restoring 3 lakes in South Bengaluru covering over 44 acres that affects the nearby area with floods and if rejuvenated will impact the connected lakes and land, to make it more liveable and sustainable.

    Partnering with Green Schools

    RCB aims to reach the champions of tomorrow and drive self-driven green initiatives with the primary aim of driving long term behavioural change by partnering with Schools in Bengaluru.

    Make RCB Carbon Neutral

    To offset RCB's carbon footprint, solar energy and wind energy will be used to power the stadiums during the home matches. RCB will request fans pledge their carbon offset to help us collectively make RCB carbon neutral.

    Help RCB Offset Carbon Footprint by pledging your use of renewable energy. Our auditors will reach out to you to confirm your participation.
